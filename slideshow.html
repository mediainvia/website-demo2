Project Description


My name is Diego Morales and I’m a Brazilian Visual Designer, currently working as Senior Art Director at Buck. In my 10+ years of experience, I’ve worked with clients from a wide range of industries, designing ownable and human-centric visual systems with polished craftsmanship and high attention to detail. At the moment my role consists in directing multiple teams to elevate both, new and consolidated brands, as well as creating meaningful digital experiences that will help our clients create deep connections with their users.
